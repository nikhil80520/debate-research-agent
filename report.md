## Query
Is coffee good for health?

## Pro Summary
Moderate coffee consumption (3-4 cups per day) may have health benefits, such as reducing the risk of stroke, type 2 diabetes, and certain types of cancer, according to the Harvard School of Public Health.

## Con Summary
Moderate coffee consumption may be beneficial for overall health, but sensitivity to caffeine can affect individual results.
